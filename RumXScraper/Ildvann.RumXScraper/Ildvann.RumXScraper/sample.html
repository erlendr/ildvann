Smith & Cross Traditional Jamaican Rum, crafted by the Hampden distillery in Jamaica, is a potent blend known for its bold ester profile and fruity notes. Distilled from molasses using a pot still, this rum is aged for 3 years and bottled at 57% ABV. The aroma is dominated by banana, esters, and tropical fruit, while on the palate, it's characterized by distinct banana, tropical fruit, and a funky, dry finish. Though appreciated for its rich flavor and excellent price-to-value ratio, the high alcohol content can be intense for some, making it a versatile choice for cocktails or adventurous sipping. Users highlight its Jamaican funk and mixability, positioning it as a staple in both home bars and mixology.
